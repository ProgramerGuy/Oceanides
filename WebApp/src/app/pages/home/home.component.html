How important are our oceans?

Our ocean produces more than 50% of the world's oxygen and is capable of absorbing over 50 times more carbon dioxide than our atmosphere. Not only that, 70% of our oceans are capable of regulating our climate and weather patterns, it helps fish to move, and creates new ecosystems. This brings us great benefits, such as food, medicines and economical benefits.

Ocean is the home of over 700,000 marine species, about 284,300 km2 of reef extensions.
